Tutorial: How to Use ON Stage'd

  1. Select an audio source: Use one of the two first buttons to choose between an available audio or your own audio.
  2. Choose a room: Select a room category and then a specific room from the dropdown menus.
  3. Adjust the settings: Use the sliders and controls to set the reverb intensity and the volume.
  4. Apply reverb and hear : Click the "PLAY" button to hear the effect and start the recording.
  5. Stop audio: Use the "STOP / SAVE" button to stop playback at any time. Make sure to stop the audio before launching another process.
  6. Download the processed audio: After clicking on "STOP / SAVE" the recording should be available at download throught a pop-pup "DOWNLOAD" button.
